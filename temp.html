3.Poori Recipes

Description

Poori is a popular Indian deep-fried bread made from whole wheat flour. It is soft on the inside, crispy on the outside, and commonly served with potato masala, chana masala, or vegetable kurma.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • ¾ cup water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps:-

  1. In a large bowl, add whole wheat flour and salt.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon oil and mix well.
  3. Gradually add water and knead into a firm dough.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into small equal-sized balls.
  6. Roll each ball into a small round disc using a rolling pin.
  7. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium-high heat
  8. Carefully slide one poori into the hot oil.
  9. Gently press it with a slotted spoon until it puffs up.
  10. Flip and fry the other side until golden.
  11. Remove and drain excess oil on paper towels
  12. Repeat for the remaining dough.
  13. Serve hot with potato masala or kurma.
