2.idli with sambar recipes

Description

Idli with sambar is a classic South Indian breakfast. Idlis are soft, steamed rice cakes served with hot and flavorful lentil-based vegetable sambar.

Ingredients

For idli :-

  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Water as needed

For sambar:-

  • ½ cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 drumstick, chopped (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons sambar powder
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 3 cups water

For Tempering:-

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 dried red chili
  • A few curry leaves
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)

Steps for how to make it

idli:-

  1. Wash and soak the idli rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the urad dal into a smooth batter.
  3. Grind the rice into a slightly coarse batter
  4. Mix both batters together and add salt.
  5. Leave the batter to ferment overnight or for 8-12 hours.
  6. Grease the idli molds lightly with oil.
  7. Pour the batter into the molds.
  8. Steam for 10-12 minutes until the idlis are cooked.
  9. Remove and keep aside.

For sambar:-

  1. Wash and pressure cook the toor dal until soft.
  2. Mash the cooked dal and set aside.
  3. In a pot, add onions, tomatoes, vegetables, turmeric, and water.
  4. Cook until the vegetables become tender.
  5. Add tamarind paste, salt, and sambar powder.
  6. Add the mashed dal and mix well.
  7. Simmer for 10 minutes.

For Tempering:-

  1. Heat oil in a small pan.
  2. Add red chili, curry leaves, and asafoetida.
  3. Pour the tempering into the sambar and mix well.
